### What's Broken ❌

**Current:** "Stop Guessing—Use the $5 AI Prompt Pack That Works Every Time"

**Problems:**
- "Works every time" sounds like spam
- No credibility or proof
- Doesn't explain WHAT you get
- No emotional hook

**Impact:** Low conversion rate

- Generic quotes
- No numbers/results

#### 9. POOR MOBILE CTAs
**Current:** Small buttons, same layout as desktop

**Problems:**

#### 10. NO TRUST SIGNALS
**Current:** No security badges, no guarantee mentioned

**Problems:**
